Features

Minigames

Play fun little games directly on your server - ideal for passing the time and community building!

Moderation

Automated moderation with commands such as /kick, /ban, /mute, /prison with tools to protect your server and more.

Server Design Tools

We have a custom embed builder that can be used to personalize various functions of the bot, such as the welcome message or verification process.

Birthdays

Manage your community's birthdays, with reminders, automatic congratulations and birthday gifts for your users. Everything customizable!

Level system

Engage your community with a fully customizable leveling system. Reward activity with roles, perks, or other incentives to keep your server active and fun!

Self Roles

Let your members personalize their experience by assigning themselves roles. Perfect for organizing interests, pings and more.

And much more...
